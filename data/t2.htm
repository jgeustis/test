Orasi Training

As technology complexities continue to expand, our approach to software development and testing must keep pace. Based on myriad development methodologies, it is critical that testers adjust to whatever approach the projects they are involved in use. Orasi is here to help by offering process and product training classes, taught either on-site or in a virtual, instructor-led environment through the Orasi University.
